# Geometric Camera Calibration - DUNR

This document describes the geometric camera calibration carried out for all sensors in Dunrobin House (DUNR), including interior- and relative orientation parameters, methodology, equipment and an index of the supporting documents contained in this folder.

## Method:

Geometric camera calibration method(s) used below:

- **3D Calibration Field**

**Description:** Results generate with a 3D test field, comprised of custom thermal-specif survbey markers. Processed using Australis. Please refer to associated files below for more details re: method and accuracy.
